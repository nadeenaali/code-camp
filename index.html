The purpose

The purpose of this website is to help both children and high school students to benefit. Tutoring has been shown to increase test scores by 12% because you get a deeper understanding of the subjects you are learning. Even if the children aren't struggling, that could give them a huge confidence booster. High school students often need volunteer hours for various reasons, such as graduation requirements or college applications. Providing them with an opportunity to earn these hours through tutoring can be very valuable.

In conclusion, tutoring/babysitting younger children can be a rewarding experience for high school students. It allows them to develop skills in communication, patience, and teaching while also fostering a sense of responsibility, while children are meeting new people and getting their grades higher.
